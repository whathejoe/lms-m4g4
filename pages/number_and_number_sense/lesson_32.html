Lesson 32: Visualizing Addition and Subtraction of Similar Fractions Explore and Discover!

Joseph and his mother bought a cake near Sto. Domingo Church. They divided the cake into eight equal parts. They ate 2/8 and brought the rest home.

Therefore, Joseph and his mother brought home 3/4 cake.

Fractions with the same denominator are called similar fractions.

Study how you add similar fractions.
