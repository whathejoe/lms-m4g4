Study the results below:

Note that:

In the fraction 11/12, the numerator is smaller than the denominator. It is a fraction less than 1. It is a proper fraction.

In the fraction 5/4, the numerator is bigger than the denominator. It is a fraction greater than 1. It is an improper fraction.

A fraction whose numerator is equal to the denominator is also an improper fraction. It is a fraction equal to 1. An example is 5/5

The fraction 32/7 contains a whole number and a fraction. It is a mixed fraction
