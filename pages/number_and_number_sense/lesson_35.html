Lesson 35: Visualizing Addition and Subtraction of Dissimilar Fractions Explore and Discover!

Ellen received 7/9 of a chocolate bar. When John asked for some, she gave 1/3. How much was left with Ellen?

You can find the answer by using regions or illustration. One whole chocolate bar is represented by one strip.

What if Ellen gave John 1/6 more chocolate bar? How much chocolate did John receive in all?

To find the answer you need to draw regions.
