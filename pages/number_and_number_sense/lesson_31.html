Lesson 31: Changing Fractions to Lowest Terms Explore and Discover!

Nhel is helping Edward clean the backyard. They spend 2 hours doing this every Saturday morning.

What part of the day do they spend cleaning the backyard? Express your answer in lowest term.

How will you solve the problem?

THINK: 2 hours out of 24 hours

In fraction, it is written as 2/24

To change 2/24 to its lowest term, you do this:

2/24 = 2/24 ÷ 2 = 1/12

The divisor 2 is the greatest common factor of 2 and 24.

That means, Nhel and Edward spend 1/12 of the day cleaning the backyard. A fraction is in its lowest term if the numerator and denominator have no other common factor except 1.

Both 1/12 and 2/24 are equivalent fractions. Equivalent fractions are fractions with the same size.
