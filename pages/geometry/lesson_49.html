What geometric figure does each hand represent?

Ray AB meets with ray AC at point A. When two rays meet at a common endpoint, they form an angle. Point A, the common endpoint, is called the vertex.

An angle can be named in 3 ways:

∠BAC is read as "angle BAC"

∠CAB is read as "angle CAB"

∠A is read as "angle A" or ∠1 is read as "angle 1"

An angle is measured in degrees (°). Angles can be classified according to their measurements.

A right angle measure 90°. It forms a square corner.

An acute angle measures less than 90°. It's smaller than a right angle.

An obtuse angle measures more than 90° but less than 180°.
