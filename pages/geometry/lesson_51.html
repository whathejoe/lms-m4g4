Lesson 51: Identifying and Describing Triangle Explore and Discover!

Mr. Medes showed a big triangle to his class.

He has to divide it into small triangles of different sizes.

Look at how he divided it.

How many small triangles do we have? Are the sides and angles of each triangle equal?

Triangles can be classified according to their angles.

A right triangle has a right angle which measures 90°. ΔABC is a right triangle.

An acute triangle has 3 acute angles, each measuring less than 90°. ΔDEF is an acute triangle.

An obtuse triangle has an obtuse angle which measures more than 90°. ΔGHI is an obtuse triangle

Triangles can be classified according to their sides.

An equilateral triangle has 3 equal sides. ΔJKL is an equilateral triangle.

An isosceles triangle has 2 equal sides. ΔMNO is an isosceles triangle.

A scalene triangle has no equal sides. ΔPQR is a scalene triangle.
