Chapter 5: Number Patterns and Equations

You see patterns in things or objects around you. The activities that you do every day follow a pattern. The way you arrange your toys or things in a cabinet follows a pattern. Patterns take many forms. In art , decorations may be combined or repeated to form patterns . The structure of a building or how the windows are built , suggest patterns. In the bodies of animals and even in leaves and branches of trees , patterns can be observed. Numbers are used to form patterns. When you skip count, you are creating a pattern.

This chapter will provide you with lessons and activities on number patterns. It includes lessons on determining missing terms in a sequence of even or odd numbers, and multiplies or factors of a number , etc. The lesson on number patterns is extended to finding the missing number in a number sentence which is a pre-algebra concept.
