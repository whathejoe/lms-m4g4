Mrs. Arce begins cleaning their house at 9:10am. She expects to finish her work at 9:55am. How long will Mrs. Arce do the cleaning?

What is asked? The time Mrs. Arce clean their house.

What are the given? 9:55AM (Time Finished) and 9:10am (Time Begins)

What is the operation to be used? Subtraction

What is the number sentence? 9:55 – 9:10 = N

What is the answer? Mrs. Arce clean thier house about 45 minutes.

The grade 4 pupils of Ms. Alma took their lunch right after their class at 11:45 am. If they finished eating at 12:30pm how long did they take their lunch?

What is asked? The time of grade 4 did take their lunch.

What are the given? 11:45am (time started) and 12:30pm (time finished)

What is the operation to be used? Subtraction

What is the number sentence? 12:30 – 11:45 = N

What is the answer? 45 minutes did grade 4 pupils take their lunch.
